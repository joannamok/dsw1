Extra Cheesy Spinach Dip Recipe

Ingredients

  • One 8 oz package of cream cheese
  • One 8 oz package of sour cream
  • 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup shredded white cheddar
  • 4 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 1 ts salt
  • 1/2 ts fresh ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Set aside 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese for topping.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream, shredded cheeses, salt and pepper. 4.Mix ingredients together well.
  4. Add spinach and continue to mix.
  5. Pour ingredients into a Pyrex baking dish, sprinkle remaining Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake in oven for 45 minutes.
